\paragraph{Contributions.}
\begin{itemize}
    \item A joint conditional-flow model for right-censored survival data with
          closed-form likelihood, survival, and density.
    \item A differentiable Soft--Nelson--Aalen Wasserstein regularizer that
          augments the censored MLE with a population-level distributional
          constraint.
    \item A four-scenario simulation study showing the proposed flow strictly
          dominates Kaplan--Meier, Cox PH, parametric AFT, and a parametric
          LogNormal-flow ablation on $T$-side fit.
\end{itemize}
